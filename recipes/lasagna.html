Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and plenty of melted cheese.r The hearty filling typically combines ground beef or sausage with tomatoes, garlic, onions, and herbs, creating a savory sauce that is nestled between tender pasta layers.

Once assembled, the lasagna is baked until golden and bubbly, with the cheese forming a deliciously crisp top. The result is a comforting, flavorful dish perfect for family dinners, offering a satisfying balance of textures and tastes in every bite.

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion, Chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • MOzzarella cheese, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, then sauté chopped onion and minced garlic until soft.
  3. Add ground beef to the pan and cook until browned; drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, salt, black pepper, and simmer for 10 to 15 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta (or cottage) cheese with an optional egg for creaminess.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan; repeat layers.
  8. Finish with a layer of noodles topped with sauce and plenty of mozzarella and Parmesan.
  9. Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25 to 30 minutes; uncover and bake 10 more minutes until golden.
  10. Let the lasagna rest for 10 to 15 minutes before slicinh and serving
