Description

Burgers are a popular and satisfying dish made with a juicy ground beef patty, typically seasoned with salt and pepper, and grilled or pan-fried to perfection. The patty is placed inside a soft bun and layered with classic toppings like lettuce, tomato, onions, cheese, and pickles, creating a delicious mix of flavors and textures.

Often served with condiments such as ketchup, mayonnaise, or mustard, burgers are enjoyed as a quick meal or at gatherings with friends and family. Whether kept simple or stacked with extras like bacon, avocado, or fried eggs, burgers remain a timeless favorite across many cultures.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef( or beef patties)
  • BUrger buns
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Cheese slices
  • lettuce
  • Tomato,sliced
  • Onion, sliced
  • pickles
  • ketchup, mayonnaise, or mustard

Steps

  1. Season the ground beef with salt and black pepper, then form into patties.
  2. Heat a pan or grill over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook the patties for about 3 to 4 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired doneness.
  4. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  5. Toast the burger buns lightly on the grill or in a pan.
  6. Spread ketchup, mayonnaise, or mustard on the buns as desired.
  7. Place the cooked patty on the bottom half of each bun.
  8. Add lettuce, tomato slices, onion slices, and pickles on top of the patty.
  9. Cover with the top bun.
  10. Serve hot with fries or a side of your choice.
